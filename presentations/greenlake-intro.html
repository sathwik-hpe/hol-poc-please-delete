What is HPE GreenLake?

HPE GreenLake is a cloud services platform that brings the cloud experience to your apps and data wherever they are.

  • Cloud experience anywhere - edge, data center, or colocations
  • Pay-per-use pricing model
  • No upfront capital investment required
  • Full control over your data and infrastructure

The Cloud Dilemma

Public Cloud

  • Easy to start
  • Scalable on demand
  • Pay as you go
  • But... unpredictable costs
  • Security concerns
  • Data gravity issues

On-Premises

  • Full control
  • Data stays local
  • Predictable costs
  • But... high upfront cost
  • Slow to scale
  • Complex to manage

GreenLake: Best of Both Worlds

Cloud Experience + On-Prem Control

Get cloud agility and pay-per-use pricing while keeping your data and apps where you need them

  • Cloud-like flexibility with on-premises security
  • Pay only for what you use, scale as you grow
  • No cloud data egress fees
  • Meet compliance and data residency requirements
